Things To Do And Not To Do On Social Media

Things to do on media

You should use a good picture and write a clean bio especially on websites like LinkedIn. You should think before you post something or reply to someone when you are arguing. You should use the privacy settings to control who can see your photos and the posts you are tagged in. You should check the news or information before you share it with your friends in your batch. You should talk to people who're interested in Computer Science and Cloud Computing to make friends who can help you in your career.

Things not to do on media

You should not post bad things or things that can hurt people and can be seen by the people who may give you a job in the future.

You should not share where you live or your phone number, with everyone.

You should not use language when you talk to your teachers or the official pages of your university.

You should not tag your friends in photos or videos without asking them

You should not click on links that look suspicious or give your password to apps that say they can show you who looked at your profile.
