PREVENTION GUIDE CHECKLIST

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. You should never give your UPI PIN or OTP to anyone no matter how urgent the request seems.

2. Always check the website address and the person who is sending you something before you click on any links or download files.

3. You should turn on two-factor authentication on all your accounts if you can.

4. Make sure your phone and the apps on it have the security updates.

5. It is not an idea to use public Wi-Fi when you are doing online banking or making payments.

6. Use a strong password for each of your online accounts and think about using a password manager.

7. Regularly save a copy of your files to a cloud or an external drive.

8. Be careful of messages that say you have won a prize or can get a refund. Always check with the people first.

9. If you think someone is trying to trick you or you see something report it right away, to:

National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

Helpline: 1930
